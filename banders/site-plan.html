What is Banders?

Bander’s is a growth company, we are focused in Asian street food, we have the best Korean Corn Dogs and Korean wings, and we are always searching for the best products for our customers. our ingredients 100% from Asia, give us the certainty that we are bringing the best to our customers. Banders is always growing, and we will do the right things to achieve or mission.

Below you can see our vision and Mission.

Mission: Give every customer exceptional good quality food, and special moments with family and friends.

Vision: Being a great and comfortable space that the customers can enjoy.

What makes us different?: We are always trying to deliver a perfect corndog at time, followed by a great smile, and we are sure that in every place in what you taste banders would be an excellent moment.

Our Products: Or specialty is the great Bander Kruji, made with our special Bander dough and panko, to make it really tasty and crispy, and in our wings, we have the amazing sweet and spicy flavor that is a explosion of flavor in our customers with a delicious taste of sweet but a little spicy. And we have our Banders fries, and our Bander Smoothies, that can make everyone to have a good moment.

About us

History of banders: We founded banders because we wanted to bring something new to or community, a thing that can be delicious and everybody can enjoy and could have a different experience than before, and we want to expand and bring this type of experience to new people new communities, and so on.

Founders: The founders of banders were Luis Eduardo, Jose Emanuel, and Kim Josell, together they decided to believe in something different than the local food and taking the risks they set everything to create banders.

The first Local of banders: In May of 2023 Banders Begins in Cuernavaca Morelos Mexico, in a tiny space in the street calzada de los reyes, with no more than a frier and the ingredients to make each corn dog special, for the customers. With time and patience, the name of banders began to spread in the whole town, lately unfortunately they needed to close in this place, but Luis Jose and Kim didn’t give up, and they started to make plans to open in another place and continue with banders.

Kim Josell: Kim Josell born in March 2001 Kim is the son of Helga and Alberto, he is studying in BYU-Pathway worldwide as a full stack developer, since August 2020, with his organizational skills he give to banders the structure as a founders with standardization and systematization of the systems in banders.

Luis Eduardo: ( I don’t have some information yet) with his creativity he created the concept and logo and all the idea of banders give to banders the special touch that mark a difference.

Jose Emanuel: Jose Emanuel born in August 2002, Jose is the son of Helga and Alberto, he is a missionary of the church of Jesus Christ of the latter-day saints, with his selling talents and his especial treatment with people he give the connections and made the enough efforts to make grow banders.
